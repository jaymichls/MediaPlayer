Movie Poster Taglines

Welcome to the Movie Poster Tagline Database, where you can search or browse through our large collection of taglines. We are slowly adding taglines for all English language posters on the site. At the moment we have 10,128 taglines for 6,170 films including almost every film going back to 1998. We are adding more taglines each day and hope to complete the collection within a few months.

If you are not familiar with what taglines are, just think of phrases like "In space no one can hear you scream", "Just when you thought it was safe to go back in the water", or "Why so serious?" An attempt to capture the essence of a film or describe its plot in a handful of words.

If you are looking for a particular tagline, type a few words into the search box below. Use quotation marks around words to search for specific phrases. If you're just browsing, click on one of the letters below to check out taglines that start with that letter. Or, if you prefer, check out the various tagline award winners from recent years.
